R&D Internship | Hapticmedia | June to August 2018

My mission was to visualy enhance the proprietary renderer by working on surface reflexion or lighting. I chose the latter. I ended up working on an implementation of the Screen Space Directional Occlusion, to which I added some functionnality.

  • Compiled several research papers in order to select a suitable technology to develop
  • Programed a C++/OpenGL prototype to implement a first version of the SSDO
  • Enhanced the technology by adding global illumination support in real-time
  • Implemented the complete algorithm in the company's render engine in Babylon.js
  • Wrote a fully documented document about my reserch, development and improvements for Hapticmeda to work on

Blender Educator | 2017 & 2018

  • Animated a course about Blender at the University of Strasbourg.
